# 🌿 EcoRunner

## Purpose & Objective

**EcoRunner** is an interactive educational game designed to teach players about environmental responsibility and waste management through engaging gameplay. The project aims to raise awareness about recycling and proper waste disposal while making learning fun and accessible to all ages.

### Core Objectives:
- **Educate** players on the importance of recycling and waste sorting
- **Inspire** sustainable habits through gamified learning
- **Empower** communities to take action in environmental conservation
- **Make** eco-education entertaining and interactive

---

## Project Overview

EcoRunner is a fast-paced, browser-based game where players collect falling trash items in a 30-second collection phase, then sort them into the correct recycling bins. The game combines quick reflexes with environmental knowledge, creating a unique learning experience that's both educational and entertaining.

### How It Works:

1. **🏃 Collection Phase** - Players control a character that moves left and right to collect falling trash items within a 30-second time limit. Each item collected earns points and contributes to the sorting phase.

2. **♻️ Sorting Phase** - After collection ends, players transition to a dedicated sorting interface where they must drag and drop collected items into the correct recycling bins (Plastic, Paper, Glass, Food Waste).

3. **📊 Results** - The game grades the player's sorting accuracy and displays an eco-fact for each incorrect sort, reinforcing learning through immediate feedback.

---

## Game Features

### Educational Content
- **4 Waste Categories**: Plastic, Paper, Glass, and Food Waste
- **Eco-Facts**: Engaging environmental facts displayed after each sort attempt
- **Real-World Impact**: Learn facts like "Plastic takes 400+ years to decompose" and "Glass can be recycled endlessly"

### Gameplay Mechanics
- **Character Selection**: Choose between two unique eco-warriors (Alex or Maya)
- **Timed Collection**: 30-second intense collection phase with dynamic trash spawning
- **Drag-and-Drop Sorting**: Intuitive interface for sorting trash into correct bins
- **Score System**: Earn points for successful collections and accurate sorts
- **Grading System**: Receive performance grades (S, A, B, C) based on sorting accuracy

### Visual Design
- **Retro Pixel Art**: Nostalgic 8-bit inspired graphics and animations
- **Parallax Scrolling**: Dynamic background movement for immersive gameplay
- **Clear Visual Feedback**: Animations and effects show correct/incorrect sorts

## Getting Started

### How to Play:

1. **Open the Game** - Navigate to `index.html` in your browser
2. **Select Your Character** - Choose between Alex or Maya
3. **Click Start** - Begin the 30-second collection phase
4. **Move & Collect** - Use arrow keys or A/D to move, SPACE or UP arrow to jump
5. **Collect Trash** - Run into falling trash items to collect them
6. **Sort the Trash** - When time's up, drag items into the correct bins
7. **View Your Results** - See your grade and sorting accuracy
8. **Learn & Replay** - Read eco-facts and play again to improve!

### Controls:
- **← →** or **A/D** - Move left/right
- **SPACE** or **↑** - Jump
- **Mouse Drag** - Drag trash items to bins (sorting phase)
